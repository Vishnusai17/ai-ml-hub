# 🤝 Contributing to AI/ML Mastery Hub

Thank you for helping make this resource better! Every contribution counts.

---

## What You Can Contribute

- ✅ A course or tutorial you found helpful
- ✅ A research paper summary
- ✅ A correction or updated link
- ✅ A new section (e.g., Reinforcement Learning, MLOps, Computer Vision)

---

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Create a branch**: `git checkout -b add/your-resource-name`
3. **Make your change** following the format below
4. **Commit**: `git commit -m "add: [resource name] to [section]"`
5. **Push**: `git push origin add/your-resource-name`
6. **Open a Pull Request** — describe what you added and why it's valuable

---

## Format for Adding a Resource

For a **course or tutorial**, add to the relevant table:

```markdown
| Course Name | Platform | Free? |
|---|---|---|
| Your Course | Provider | ✅ / Paid |
```

For a **research paper**, add to `research-papers/README.md`:

```markdown
#### [Paper Title (Year)](https://arxiv.org/abs/XXXX)
**Authors:** Author et al. (Institution)
**Summary:** 2-3 sentence plain-English explanation.
**Key idea:** The core technical contribution in one sentence.
**Why it matters:** Why this paper is important to know about.
**Impact:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
```

---

## Guidelines

- Keep descriptions **concise and jargon-free**
- Prefer **free or open-access** resources where possible
- Verify all **links are working** before submitting
- Papers should be from **arxiv, top conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CVPR, ACL)** or equivalent

---

Open an [Issue](https://github.com/Vishnusai17/ai-ml-hub/issues) and we'll help you get started.
